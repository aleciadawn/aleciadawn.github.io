True Career Solutions Website


True Career Solutions is a career coaching and consulting company in New York City, who needed a clean and professional site to showcase their services and manage their career development blog.
Role
Due to my previous experience working for a career development organiztion, I occupied a unique space that proved advantageous for the client. We were able to perform detailed user interviews and testing, and were able to efficiently conduct a competitive analysis.
Personas
Personas were an important part of this project. Because of the nature of the business, we wanted to make sure we were illustrating the bredth of my client's services as effectively as possible. Career transitions tend to accompany very personal and complex points in a person's life. I built personas based on ther user surveys and interviews we conducted, and constantly referred back to them throughout the design, development, and testing process.
