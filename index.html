About

I'm the Lead Designer at Dealer360 where I specialize in building UX solutions for complex data consumption.

I love creating simple, beautiful designs; and more than anything else, I love solving problems.

My passion for digital product design stems from my fascination with the human experience and how we interact with the world around us. I believe the integration of utility and beauty found in quality design allows us to elevate our interaction with the digital world, and I strive to elevate our reality with every experience I design.

In another life, I spent two years as a Peace Corps volunteer in Paraguay where I managed national initiatives in youth leadership and gender equality. Following my service, I moved to New York to run the volunteer and corporate partnership programs at a workforce development non-profit working with immigrant professionals.

I’m also an unofficial yogi, aspiring runner, bourbon enthusiast, and collector of plants.
