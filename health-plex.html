HEALTH PLEX FITNESS CENTER

WEB DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT | REBRANDING

DESCRIPTION

The client was a local gym looking to modernize their website and branding. The were looking for a cleaner, easy-to-use, research-based website that would help drive membership and appeal to a younger audience.

SOFTWARE

Google Surveys, Balsamiq, Photoshop, Illustrator, Sketch, InVision

PROCESS

Research: User Surveys, Personas, User Stories
Design: Site Map & User Flows, Low & High Fidelity Wireframes, InVision Prototype
Testing: Usability Tests

RESEARCH

USER SURVEYS AND INTERVIEWS

The first step in the process was discussing goals and limitations of the client. Next, I sent out a survey to both members and non-members. The survey received resoponses from 12 non-members and six members. Of the non-members surveyed, three did not have any type of gym membership. Based on the findings of the survey along with 3 personal interviews, I anazlyzed the original site to identify the main pain pain points the client was experiencing with their site and made sure to prioritze those points through the remander of my design process

RESEARCH HIGHLIGHTS

  • 74% of members surveyed highlighted staff as one of their favorite aspects of the health club.


  • 83% of users noted facility cleanliness as one of their top priorities when chosing a fitness facility.


  • 56% of users surveyed claimed a modern facility and equipment were among their top three priorities when deciding to join a gym.


  • 100% of users said they would consider switching gyms based on reviews and/or friend recomendations.

PAIN POINTS

  • The original site was missing a staff page and didn't contain any information on ownership or staff qualificaitons.


  • The original site lacked a variety of photos, especially of high concern areas such as locker rooms, sauna, etc.


  • In addition to a lack of photo variety, the original photos were dated, not accuratly reflecting the facitly.


  • Despite the signifcant number of positive reviews on Google, Yelp, and Facebook, there were no testimonials quoted or linked on the website.
